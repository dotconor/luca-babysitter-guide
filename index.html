A guide for Denise — from Conor & Rach 💛

Daily Rhythm

  • 10 AM arrive · Noon lunch · 1 PM nap
  • Milk before nap, snack after nap
  • We relieve at 6 PM, except Wednesday when it’s 7 PM

Meals & Drinks

  • Milk in sippy cup; water available all day. Bottles are on the far right side of the kitchen against the wall, and in the cabinet across from the dishwasher.
  • His plates are in the second drawer from the top
  • Lunch: tofu + tomato sauce, rice/quinoa, pizza, quesadilla, eggs, chickpeas/peas, avocado, veggies
  • Snacks: apple/crackers + peanut butter, fruit, granola bars, yogurt

Nap

Usually around 1 PM
  • Close blinds, read 1–2 books, milk, stuffed animals
  • Milk is easiest on the walk home before nap

Activities

  • Loves books, songs, toys, coloring, park, sprinklers, friends
  • Park toys on first floor: bucket, bus, dump truck
  • Park toys are for trading — he’s good at it

No Screens

  • We don’t do screens with him — just books, toys, and sometimes music
  • For music say: “Ok Google, please play the Wheels on the Bus by CoComelon”
  • It usually keeps playing kid music after

If He’s Having a Hard Time

  • Try snack, Baby Maisy, “Wheels on the Bus,” book, or cuddle
  • Walk outside / change scenery / toy distraction can reset him
  • Calm voice + patience usually works best
